Crystal Collector Game!

A random number is chosen by the computer. Your goal is to get that same number by clicking on the crystals. Each crystal has a value that is unknown until you click on that crystal and see how much has been added to your total. If you match the computer's number, you have won. If your total is greater than the number, you have lost. Each crystal's value will be changed after a win or a loss.
